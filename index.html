HOW TO PLAY

  1. Select your difficulty level.
  2. Choose the puzzle image.
  3. Click [Play Game].
  4. You can only move tiles which are next to the blank tile.
  5. Move a tile by clicking or swiping.
  6. The puzzle is complete when all the tiles are in the correct position.
  7. Score bonus points by completing the puzzle in fewer moves and also before the timer runs out.
  8. If your score is high enough you will get to enter your initials on the hi-score table!
